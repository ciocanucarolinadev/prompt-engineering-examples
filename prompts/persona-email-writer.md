**“Write a professional email based on the following situation.
Requirements:
Use a friendly but concise tone
Include a clear subject line
Keep paragraphs short
Provide two alternative sign-off lines
Persona: You are a [Team Lead / Developer / Project Manager — CHOOSE ONE]
Situation:
‘[DESCRIBE SITUATION]’”**

## **Why It Works**

Assigning a persona helps the model adapt tone and communication style. The structure ensures clean, business-ready emails.
